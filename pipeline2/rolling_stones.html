It concentrates on the 2020 updated list, on which some new albums were added, while others were up- or downrated, or entirely removed. The "Maj. contrib." lists up to three main contributors. These also include copyeditors and blocked/banned users, but not GA reviewers. To avoid any conflicts, you may note under that column that you are working on one or several specific articles.
